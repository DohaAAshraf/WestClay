    West Clay Compound – A Luxurious Residential Community in 6th of October City

    West Clay Compound is a prestigious residential project developed by Remal Real Estate Development Company, offering a modern and upscale living experience in 6th of October City. Spanning 10 acres, the compound combines contemporary architectural designs with vast green spaces and high-end facilities to provide residents with a comfortable and secure environment.

    Prime Location in 6th of October City

    • ⭐ Sphinx International Airport – A short drive away, facilitating travel and connectivity.
    • ⭐ Mall of Egypt & Mall of Arabia – Two of the largest shopping and entertainment centers in Egypt.
    • ⭐ 6th October University & Nile University – Providing top-tier education options for residents.
    • ⭐ Wahat Road & 26th of July Corridor – Ensuring easy transportation across Cairo.
    • ⭐ 1 minute from major road: Dahshour Link.

    Project Area

    WESTCLAY spans 25 acres, covered with green spaces and water features.

    Residential Units & Pricing

    West Clay offers a variety of unit types, including:

    • Apartments & Duplexes
    • Sizes: Units start from 100 square meters, with duplexes ranging up to 240 square meters.
    • Prices: Starting at 5,500,000 EGP.
    • Payment Plan: Flexible options with 10% down payment and installments over 7 years.

    Modern Design & Green Spaces

    • Designed by renowned engineering consultant Mohamed Talaat, West Clay emphasizes a balance between modern architecture and nature. The compound features expansive green areas, landscaped gardens, and water features, creating a tranquil and refreshing atmosphere. The sustainable design incorporates alternative energy sources, ensuring an eco-friendly and healthy living environment.
